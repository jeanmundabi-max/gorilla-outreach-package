The MHHS October 2026 Deadline Creates a Closing Window

The MHHS migration is live. October 2026 is the 80% milestone. Any mid-market UK energy supplier with legacy settlement data infrastructure faces a compounding problem: the 48x increase in daily data volume per meter is already generating settlement errors. Those errors become imbalance charges. Imbalance charges eat the margin. The CFO notices when the monthly reconciliation produces a different number from the trading team's forecast.

Mid-market suppliers with 50,000 to 500,000 customers are in the worst position. They are large enough that the data volume is painful. They are not large enough to have the internal engineering team to build a proper solution. They are the Gorilla buyer.

The window is five months. After October 2026, the migration is past its peak urgency. Companies that did not fix their data infrastructure by then will have either committed to an in-house build or accepted the settlement losses as normal operating cost.
