Execution Plan

30-Day Playbook

The sequence for turning the targeting database into qualified conversations with mid-market energy retailers.

Week 1

Finalise and Confirm

Confirm decision-maker contacts for all 15 Tier 1 companies. Identify which companies Gorilla already has a relationship with. Separate those into an expansion track rather than cold outreach.

Week 2

Sequence Launch

Signal-triggered emails go to the Tier 1 list. LinkedIn DM sequence starts in parallel. Every email references the specific signal that flagged the company — job posting, MHHS milestone, or director change. No generic templates.

Week 3

Qualify and Brief

First replies qualified by urgency. Pre-call intelligence briefs prepared for any company that responds positively. Gorilla's commercial team receives the brief before every call — MHHS status, leakage estimate, decision-maker profile, recommended product entry point.

Week 4

Review and Improve

Which signal type generated the highest reply rate? MHHS migration? Job postings? Ofgem enforcement? The answer determines the weighting for month 2. The outreach system adapts based on what is actually converting.
