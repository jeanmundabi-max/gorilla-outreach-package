00 Executive Summary

Two prime segments. One lives and dies by a job posting. One lives and dies by a regulatory deadline.

The headline finding

Segment 01 (In-House Build energy retailers) and Segment 02 (Mid-Market Challenger Domestic Suppliers) are the only two segments where MDB's targeting database creates a live buying signal feed, not just a static prospect list. The distinguishing factor: both have observable, public data signals that confirm a specific energy retailer is in an active buying window right now — not generally interested in what Gorilla does.

Segment 01 scores the highest Outreach Priority Score because the job posting signal is the loudest and most actionable: when an energy retailer posts for a Senior Pricing Analyst or Data Engineer — Settlement, they have already validated the problem Gorilla solves. The only question is build versus buy. MDB's role is to reach them before they hire and the internal build begins. Once the engineer is in post, the buying window closes for 12 to 18 months.

The MHHS urgency engine

Both top segments have a time-bounded accelerant that does not exist in most B2B sales cycles: MHHS migration. Elexon's October 2026 milestone (80% of meters on half-hourly settlement) means every mid-market supplier not yet invested in data infrastructure is running out of runway. A pricing model that handles 30-minute settlement data does not handle 48x data volume. The October 2026 date is a hard regulatory deadline visible from public Elexon documentation — not a softly-communicated best practice.

This deadline is why Gorilla's sales cycle in these segments should compress to 60 to 90 days for the right prospects. CFOs who understand what MHHS does to their settlement costs will not wait for the next budget cycle.

10 Deployment Recommendation

Where to send Gorilla's outbound firepower first.

Step 01

Launch Segment 01 job board monitoring feed (Week 1)

Set up weekly Energy Jobline + LinkedIn Jobs + Indeed monitoring for pricing analyst, forecasting manager, and data engineer settlement roles at UK energy retailers with 50–500 employees. Build Ofgem + Companies House scoring model for Segment 02 (Week 1–2)

This database is the core targeting infrastructure for Gorilla's mid-market domestic segment.
